🎯 About

Built for people who own
their media.

Viddly is a self-hosted web app built on Node.js and Express. It wraps the powerful yt-dlp command-line tool in a clean, modern web interface — giving you the full power of yt-dlp without ever touching a terminal.

Your data stays on your machine. No cloud. No subscriptions. No tracking. Just a fast, reliable tool for downloading the videos you care about.
